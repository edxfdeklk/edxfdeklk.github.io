What is sushi

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish consisting of prepared vinegared rice, usually with some sugar and salt, and accompanied by a variety of ingredients such as seafood, usually raw, and vegetables. There are many different styles of sushi and how they are presented, but one key ingredient is "sushi rice", also known as shari or sumeshi.
