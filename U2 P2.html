How to Roll Sushi

Lay a layer of nori on a bamboo mat for the sushi.

Spread a ball of rice on the seaweed.


Place and fill with your favorite ingredients.


Hold the edge of the bamboo mat with your thumb and roll from the end of the topping that was spread first, letting the seaweed wrap the first topping.

Proceed to roll sushi. Roll slowly, making sure the entire sushi roll is the same shape and size.


To tighten the sushi roll, roll the sushi back and forth on the bamboo mat, making sure the sushi roll is tight and airtight.

Let the rolled sushi sit for a while before cutting. The rice will soften the seaweed when it sits so that it can be cut neatly when cut.


Cut the sushi rolls with a sharp, moist knife. The thickness of each piece of sushi depends on the amount of filling.


Then you can enjoy the food.
